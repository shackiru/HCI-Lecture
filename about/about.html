Our Storyline

MASAKini's journey began in December 2022, starting to serve a collection of food and drinks that were viral at the time.


Foods and drinks that were once popular usually only last for a while, then disappear and less people are selling them. This causes a negative effect on the food business in Indonesia. With the forever-changing culinary trends, many businesses have had to close because they weren't selling well.


MASAKini aims to remove the stigma of unsustainable culinary trends in Indonesia, so that it remains sustainable and can be tried all year long, from year to year. We’ll also continue to add more menu according to food trends every year.
